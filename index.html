Embrace Team

Shah Wan

Founder | Writer | Director | Producer

Shah is a writer, director, producer & actor. He started making short films in 2018 with "Stalk". His dream is to work in the film industry. As of now, he is a freelance writer and an independent filmmaker.
TWITTER LETTERBOXD

Hans Rahman

Founding Member | Actor | Writer

In 2018, Hans, a longtime friend of Shah, co-created the Embrace film-collective. He made his acting debut as the protagonist in "Stalk". He will reprise the role in the upcoming "His Persona".
TWITTER LETTERBOXD

Hunter R. Lewis

Writer | Actor | Designer

Fueled with the passion for storytelling, Hunter always had the knack for writing screenplays. He first contributed to the Embrace team by designing movie posters but has now ventured into acting. You can soon see him in a new mysterious role in the upcoming "His Persona".
TWITTER LETTERBOXD

Chris Kruczynski

Music Composer | Editor

Chris is a talented young artist specializing in music composing & video editing. His first contribution was editing the short film "Isolated Guilt". Since then, Chris' credits include post-production assistance for "The Wayward", and additional music scoring for "His Persona".
TWITTER LETTERBOXD

Egor Kabantsov

Developer | Designer

Egor first contributed to the Embrace team by designing posters for films like "Stalk" and "Hey There". He is a Frontend Developer and the creator of this website.
PORTFOLIO LETTERBOXD

Zulhakim

Founding Member | Actor | Camera Operator

Zulhakim is an important figure in the creation of this film-collective. He is the stunt coordinator & camera assistant. He will appear in the upcoming "His Persona".

Syafiq

Founding Member | Actor

Syafiq made his acting debut as the hooded vigilante in "Stalk". His other credits include additional crew assistance for "Hey There". He will reprise the role of The Stalker in "His Persona".
